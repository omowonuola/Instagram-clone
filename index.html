bolajiid Hello, friends! Today's sermon was mind-boggling powerful. If you missed or need a recap, here it is.
Topic: HOW TO HAVE RESULTS THAT DEFY LOGIC
1. First, understand Logic. Logic refers to rational, understandable, natural method of reasoning. #SundayService
2. Results that defy logic are outcomes that are outside the natural order or reasoning. #SundayService
3. Every Christian has an inbuilt capacity to produce results that no natural methodology can explain. #SundayService
4. To produce logic defying results, your Faith must be RADICAL. Meaning you are trusting God and His word even when it doesn't make sense #SundayService
5. I would have you know today that Faith is superior to logic. You can ask the people of Jericho at the time their walls fell. #SundayService
6. Noah built an ark at a time when no one had seen rain before, all because He had an instruction to do so. That's RADICAL Faith. #SundayService
7. There are some people reading this, God is telling you/will tell you to do things that has never been done before in your Industry, seems so illogical, obey Him. OBEY HIM. #SundayService
8. Peter walked on water. RADICAL Faith. It hadn't been done before. There are some of you, God will call you to step out of your comfort zone, into unchartered territories. OBEY HIM #SundayService.
9. It must have been scary for Peter, but always remember, Fear is the enemy, Faith is the ally. Faith often sounds insane or scary until the result manifests. #SundayService
10. You can't exercise Faith beyond your knowledge level. How much of God and His Word do you know? How often do you spend time just to know Him. #SundayService
11. If you don't know God, how then can you trust Him? How then can you say like Daniel and his friends, 'I KNOW my God will deliver me from this fire'. #SundayService
12. Three things I want you to remember this week: > My thoughts will limit my Faith > My thoughts control my life, but I control my thoughts > I can change how I feel, by changing what I think. #SundayService.
